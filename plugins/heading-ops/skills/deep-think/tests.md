# Test Prompts: deep-think

## Should Trigger (positive)
Realistic, messy prompts that SHOULD activate this skill.

- "think through this -- should we go direct in a new market or through a channel partner"
- "break this down for me: we can either raise now at lower valuation or wait 6 months"
- "help me reason about whether to prioritize [region A] or [region B] for Q2"
- "what are we missing in our go-to-market approach"
- "I need to think about whether Sam should lead the ExampleTelco engagement or if I should stay on it"
- "analyze this carefully -- three VCs want to talk but the terms are all different"
- "something feels off about the ExampleTelco deal, help me figure out what"
- "structured thinking on this: build vs buy for the analytics module"
- "I'm torn between hiring a CRO now or waiting until after the raise, think through the tradeoffs"
- "deep think -- should 31C position as a product company or a platform company for investors"

## Should NOT Trigger (negative)
Prompts that are similar but should NOT activate this skill.

- "what's 31C's current valuation" (factual lookup, not reasoning)
- "write the proposal for ExampleTelco" (implementation task)
- "research the Nigerian telecom market" (OSINT or market-brief)
- "how many employees do we have" (simple question)
- "schedule a call with the VCs" (calendar task)
