# Test Prompts: linkedin-post

## Should Trigger (positive)
Realistic, messy prompts that SHOULD activate this skill.

- "write a LinkedIn post about sovereign DPI and why it matters for emerging markets"
- "draft a post on our TradeExpo experience -- we had 40+ meetings"
- "LinkedIn content about the shift from deep packet inspection to deep packet intelligence"
- "post about data sovereignty for African telcos, make it punchy"
- "write something for LinkedIn about why PoV beats PoC every time"
- "I want to post about the 31C tribe culture, our no-family-no-team approach"
- "draft a LinkedIn post reacting to a competitor sanctions news"
- "post about meeting the Nigerian delegation at TradeExpo"
- "LinkedIn post -- just got back from a conference, reflections on the regional cybersecurity market"
- "write a thought leadership post about why telcos are sitting on an intelligence goldmine"

## Should NOT Trigger (negative)
Prompts that are similar but should NOT activate this skill.

- "create a 5-part LinkedIn content series on DPI" (linkedin-series, not single post)
- "draft an email to Sara" (email-draft skill)
- "send a message to the tribe about our TradeExpo success" (tribe-message skill)
- "write a blog article about sovereign DPI" (general content, not LinkedIn format)
- "generate an image for my LinkedIn post" (flux-image or image-prompt skill)
